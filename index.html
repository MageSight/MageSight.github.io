Weapon Reroll Pool

Each weapon has its own pool of rerolls, set with this control. The pool is shared across every attack made with that weapon — including the extra attacks generated by ROF d3 — and never grows with the number of attacks.

Rerolls are spent automatically where they help most: first to reroll missed attack rolls, then to improve low damage rolls. Each roll can be rerolled at most once (up to one on the attack roll and one on the damage roll per attack), and the new result is always kept — rerolls are forced, not best-of-two.

Pools are strictly per-weapon: one weapon's rerolls can never be used by another weapon's attacks. Two weapons that each have a pool are tracked separately.

Mage Sight

Version 1.0.0

Mage Sight is a probability calculator for tabletop wargaming miniatures.

Compute hit %, crit %, average damage, expected damage, and kill probability for any sequence of attacks — including abilities, rerolls, feat bonuses, armor piercing, knockdown, stationary, tough, shield guards, and more.

All calculations run entirely on-device. No data is ever collected or transmitted.
